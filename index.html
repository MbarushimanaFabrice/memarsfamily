About Us

ME MARS FAMILY

ME MARS FAMILY is a non-governmental organization that works as a charity with the aim of promoting the well-being of children and families in general.


In that purpose we are doing various activities such as helping people with disabilities, raising children from the street and returning them to their families, helping disadvantaged families to get out of poverty and to develop and preserve the environment.


We do these activities in the country of Rwanda where we are based in the Rubavu district in the Rubavu sector.

Testimonials

I'm Eric MUNYANEZA Founder of ME MARS FAMILY.

My inspiration for starting this organization is creating a world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential through equitable access to essential needs and opportunities.

So that in my life so far I have met many different philanthropists who have played a significant and positive role in my life, which made me get out of a bad life (darkness) that was not my choice and lead me to a good life (light).

The kindness of the philanthropists instilled in me a heart of unquestioning love, I am grateful to those who have been there in the past, which made me create an organization that works for the well-being of the community in general to contribute to the development of Rwanda (my country), Africa and the world in general.

I intend to making a positive and lasting impact on society by actively contributing resources, time, or expertise to address critical social issues and improve the lives of others

Let's join forces and win back to many people the confidence of their future life through good works that build the world as a whole,providing material for children taken off the streets and back to school
